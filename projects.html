Client:

 D'Farm Dream

D'Farm Dream is the first modern egg-laying poultry farm in Indonesia to implement an IoT-based system. The application will manage and monitor the entire poultry business, including operations from employees to company owners, as well as the management of chicken coops, warehouses, and finances. Additionally, the app enables investors to monitor the condition of their chickens, including health status, diseases, feeding, and overall status. This project is also aligned with the Indonesian President’s 2025-2029 initiative for providing free lunch programs.
