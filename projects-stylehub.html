StyleHub UI/UX Design and QA Engineer

A robust redesign and quality assurance effort for an AI-powered e-commerce platform, blending UI/UX improvements with manual and automated testing to deliver accurate real-time inventory, reliable recommendations, and a seamless user experience.

Role: QA Engineer & UI/UX Designer
Date: July 2024

Why I Created This Project

Throughout my work on StyleHub, an AI-powered e-commerce platform, I faced the challenge of ensuring a smooth shopping experience on a system combining personalized recommendations, real-time inventory updates, and dynamic user flows. While the platform was feature-rich, it needed improvements in usability, performance, and reliability to better support a wide range of users.

In my dual role as QA Engineer and UI/UX Designer, I focused on creating an intuitive, user-friendly interface in Figma while performing comprehensive manual and automated testing with Playwright. My goal was to deliver a shopping experience that felt seamless and engaging, with accurate recommendations and fast, error-free transactions.

This project allowed me to combine design thinking with quality assurance practices, building a foundation for an e-commerce system that is both delightful and dependable for customers.
